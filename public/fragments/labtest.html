• These paintings explore information as architecture. Through the process of selecting images, projecting selected images onto canvas, transcribing fragments of the projects, and then painting onto those transcriptions, I discover pictorial spaces­­ or as I like to call, architectures. Is a painting really so different than a graph? These paintings question this distinction. By titling the works in a faux scientific manner, my aim is to incite in my viewers’ a resistance to the authority of data representation, for there is always an architecture and the subjectivity of the architect curating the pictographic space.

Alien Objects, 2015. 6'x 3' Oil on Canvas.
